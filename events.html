Past Events (2018-19 & 2019-20)

Republic Day Parade (Jan 26)

The IETE Student Forum-VNR VJIET, in collaboration with various student chapters and clubs, organized a parade to highlight societal challenges in India. The IETE Student Chapter focused on the theme "Tribute to Indian Army," with members marching, displaying banners, and sharing messages about the sacrifices and bravery of soldiers. Faculty coordinators led the team, emphasizing the importance of recognizing and honoring the contributions of the armed forces.

National Mathematics Day (Dec 22, 2018)

Celebrated in partnership with the Health and Safety department to honor Sir Ramanujam. Events included a poster presentation on the importance of mathematics in engineering and a multi-round quiz contest. Students showcased math applications, solved problems, and participated in creative challenges. The event inspired students to appreciate the role of mathematics in engineering.

Quiz & Technical Presentation (Jan 31, 2019)

Team IETE conducted a quiz to help students develop communication, interaction, and confidence. The event included a technical presentation and problem-solving session, encouraging qualities like time management, content delivery, and overcoming stage fear. Dr. Y. Padma Sai, HOD of ECE, delivered a lecture on the growth and future of electronics, followed by student presentations on trending technologies. Judges provided guidance, and the event concluded with suggestions for different domains, motivating students to gain knowledge and experience.

National Mathematics Day (2019-20)

Students presented posters and slides on the importance and applications of mathematics in engineering. Topics included the use of matrices, Laplace and Fourier transforms, calculus, and the contributions of mathematicians like Ramanujan and Shakuntala Devi. Presentations covered practical applications such as Mobius strips, image processing, and engineering mathematics in various fields. The event concluded with awards for outstanding presentations and guidance from faculty.
